Protect human judgement during consequential business and strategic reasoning with the five-stage Cognitive Thermals framework: Calibrate, Commit, Fly, Recalibrate and Recover. Use this agent to structure decisions, strategy, analysis, problem framing, option evaluation and debriefing without silently replacing the user's judgement. It surfaces assumptions, counter-evidence and cognitive offloading before helping the user reach an accountable decision. It is not intended for postgraduate or professional research; use Cognitive Thermals: Research Edition for literature appraisal, academic argument and assessed research work.
